  • Tips
    • We need a level of sophistication/complexity of computing that we would expect as a Computer Engineer
      • We don't want to press a button and start a motor, we want something more than that.
    • FPGA's are always an additional layer of complexity that is welcome into a project
    • Human Interaction is a big thing in robotics
      • Children/Special-needs interactions
    • Robots have to have a frame of reference as to where it is
    • Underwater Bots
      • Extremely difficult to water proof a robotic platform
      • Communication underwater is difficult
      • Welding/Tubing/Plexiglass required
    • Make sure we can work on these things without too much location requirements
      • Drones can't fly on campus, you have to meet somewhere, very difficult to work with
